RE Participation App

RE Participation App is the essential private companion for real estate investors seeking to qualify for Real Estate Professional Status (REPS) or Material Participation. We built RE Participation App with a "privacy-first" philosophy. Your financial data, property details, and activity logs are yours alone—stored securely in your personal iCloud, not on our servers.

Key Features

Private & Secure

No account creation required. All data is encrypted and synced directly through your personal iCloud account. We never see, sell, or store your data.

Unlimited Evidence

Upload unlimited photos, receipts, and documents to substantiate your logs. Keep your audit trail robust without worrying about storage limits.

Effortless Logging

Quickly add logs for any property. Use the Live Activity timer on your Lock Screen to track site visits in real-time.

Progress Dashboard

Stay on top of your hour goal with clear visual breakdowns by property and activity type.

Audit-Proof Exports

Generate comprehensive CSV reports that bundle your logs with all your attached evidence photos and receipts—ready for your CPA.

Take Control of Your Tax Strategy

Track Real Estate Participation Hours Securely with Unlimited Uploads.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

3. Third Parties: We do not sell data to third parties.
